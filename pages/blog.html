My Journey in Tech

Where Passion Meets Innovation

My journey goes beyond just writing code—it's about creating a lasting impact. From mentoring young minds in web development to automating workflows that solve real-world problems, here’s how I’ve grown and contributed to the tech community.

Winning at the Google Club Hackathon

During my time at the Google Club at Moi University, I participated in a hackathon to build a portfolio website in just one day. This design, which earned me second place, was celebrated for its creativity and responsiveness. It taught me how to craft clean layouts under tight deadlines while prioritizing accessibility and user experience.

Inspiring Young Minds with Project Digital Impact

Volunteering with the Project Digital Impact Foundation allowed me to teach kids basic computer skills, creating a simple, engaging interface to help them learn. Designing layouts that fostered both creativity and ease of use remains one of my proudest achievements, and I had the chance to share it with over five schools.

Building Websites with Codejika

At Codejika, I taught secondary school students HTML and CSS and showed them how to design modern, user-friendly websites. One memorable project was creating a landing page with them—watching their excitement as their designs came to life was inspiring. It reaffirmed my passion for teaching and design.

Solving Real-World Problems with Web Automation

Automation is a core part of my skill set. This screenshot showcases a workflow I automated for scraping data from a website and populating it into a database. The clean, minimal interface was designed to simplify tracking and monitoring progress. Tasks like this save time and showcase how design and functionality work hand in hand.

Building a Community of Communicators

As an executive member of the Toastmasters Club, I played a vital role in nurturing the art of public speaking and effective communication. My contributions included helping the club grow to over 50 members, successfully launching the club, and officially registering it. This experience taught me the importance of leadership, teamwork, and fostering a supportive environment for members to develop and thrive. The club’s mission aligned with my passion for empowering individuals to become confident and articulate speakers.
