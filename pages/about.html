About Me

Front-End Developer || Web Automation Specialist

Hi, I’m Elaine — a Front-End Developer with a passion for creating websites that are more than just functional; they’re alive, intuitive, and engaging. I transform Figma designs into responsive, user-friendly websites using HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and React, with a focus on delivering seamless user experiences.
I don’t believe we should limit ourselves to traditional websites. I’m excited to explore technologies like 3D.js and Next.js to craft dynamic, immersive, and story-driven web experiences that captivate users and keep them coming back.

Beyond front-end development, I leverage Django for back-end solutions and Python for web automation, streamlining workflows and enhancing productivity. I’m also exploring how to integrate AI into software development — whether to reduce wait times, create smarter websites, or personalize user experiences.
For me, coding isn’t just about functionality — it’s about bringing designs to life, telling stories, and creating interactive experiences that inspire. Let’s connect and build something extraordinary together!
